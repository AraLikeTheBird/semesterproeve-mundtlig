How Did We Get Here

This is originally my end of semester exam. We were assigned to create a digital teaching tool to help teach a hypothetical future multimedia student who either needs to read up on the chosen subject or learn it from new. I chose to cover the subject: Character Design. I chose this subject, partially because I find it interesting and partially because it is relevant to my education after my multimedia degree. Our main obstacle for this exam was to communicate and teach about our chosen subject, using design and other creative means instead of long blocks of text. I hope this site can help people who are curious about character design.
